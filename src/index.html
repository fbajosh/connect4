About Connect 4 Trainer

Welcome to the Connect 4 Trainer, brought to you by Appmogged.

Connect 4 Trainer is a browser-first training app for studying Connect 4 openings, evaluating moves, and practicing against a solver-driven opponent.

Exact solve work runs on the end user's device in WebAssembly, and solved positions are cached in the browser with IndexedDB for reuse.

How to Play

  • Two players alternate dropping discs into the seven columns of the board.
  • Discs fall to the lowest open space in that column.
  • The first player to connect four discs horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins.
  • If the board fills without a connect four, the game ends in a draw.

Training Tools

  • Best Move: shows green hint discs in the strongest solver-backed columns for the current position.
  • Move Scores: shows the shifted solver score for each playable column above the board.
  • Performance Comparison: compares the running average quality of red and yellow moves over the current game.
